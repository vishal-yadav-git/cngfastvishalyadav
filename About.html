Our Story

In 2015, ReadyAssist was ideated as an accidental emergency assistance company by our founder, Vimal Singh SV, after losing a close friend to an accident. ReadyAssist is a family of 500+ members headquartered in a state-of-the-art facility in Bangalore, efficiently monitoring our pan-India service network through cutting-edge Autotech solutions. Today, we proudly rank among India's top-rated automotive service solution companies specializing in 24/7 emergency assistance and EV repairs.
